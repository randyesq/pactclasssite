Mrs. Featherbottom Big Wuss!

Mrs. Featherbottom is another large, feather-footed chicken not unlike Peep-Peep. The similarities end there as she is clearly on the bottom of the pecking order and no other hen in the posse is uncomfortable with chasing Mrs. Featherbottom away from a tasty morsel of food or giving her a swift peck at the neck just to harass her. Chickens can be unkind, and unfortunately, it is usually directed straight at Mrs. Featherbottom. Due to her chicken overlords' behavior to her, she remains sheepish and flighty (not in the airborn sense) to humans as well.
