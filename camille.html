Camille Sweet and Charming!

Camille brings a unique personality to the flock. She is quiet and docile when free-ranging in the yard, but when she heads into the nesting box, watch out! It can get noisy. Camille is well-known for her crazy hop-like stroll across the yard. Due to some funky feet, she can't seem to watch just right.
