Lady Gaga Just Weird!

Lady Gaga is named after her weird pop singer counterpart. In the chicken world, Lady Gaga, an Ameraucana breed, is just plain weird. With green feet, green ears, and weird-ish tufts of feather on her cheeks, she really stands out amongst the crowd. Oh, and did we mention she lays green-shelled eggs? Lady Gaga (the singer) needs a chicken worthy of her strangeness. Quite a better egg layer than expected, Lady Gaga is submissive and happy to see anyone who wants to see her.
