Lucille Great Protector!

Lucille is her own bird. Independent and strong-willed, she is known around the chicken coop for not taking any guff from any other hens. If the flock is out free-ranging and need to be corraled, be ready for a wild ride with Lucille. Undoubtedly, the most difficult chicken to get into the coop, Lucille marches to the beat of her own drum-(stick). It'll take more than chicken scratch to woo Lucille.
